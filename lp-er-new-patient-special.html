Is Our Practice Right for You?

You search for a new dentist is over! As you visit our website, we believe you will be impressed with what Dr. Le and his award-winning team at White Wood Dental have to offer. Most importantly, we think you will immediately notice the difference that comes with being treated by true professionals who care about you as a person.

Our goal is to make exceptional dentistry affordable in a convenient, family-friendly environment.

From dental emergencies to routine check-ups and cleanings to cases involving dental implants and porcelain veneers, you are in great hands. We want you to be able to relax and feel comfortable during every visit.

Dental Insurance Welcome

We realize the importance of taking care of your teeth, and we welcome dental insurance as a partner in making exceptional dentistry affordable. If you are fortunate enough to have PPO dental insurance, call us. We are an in-network partner with almost all the leading plans, and our team will work with you to maximize your benefits.

Note: If you do not see your plan, do not worry. Call. We welcome other plans.

It All Starts with You

You will be amazed at the friendliness and thoroughness of all we do. With a passion for helping people smile, our team will provide you with a complete assessment of your teeth, gums and jaw. We will take all necessary x-rays and devote the time to answer all your questions.

If a dental emergency is the reason for your visit, our first priority is to get you out of pain.

If you are visiting for the first time as a new patient, you will receive a thorough evaluation of the health of your mouth. We will screen for oral cancer and take a video tour of your teeth and gums. Also, we will evaluate the health of any existing crowns, inlays, onlays, dentures or partial dentures.

About Our Practice

When looking for someone to care for your smile, you have many choices. We do not want to be the biggest practice in North Texas. We just want to be the best. Whether your first visit is for a dental emergency or a routine check-up, we hope you and your family will become life-long patients. Give us a call today. Your smile is worth it!
